Government

A community cannot thrive without law and order. It is the responsibility of the River's Edge government to ensure all residents have a safe and fair place to call home. A panel of department directors, from agriculture to law enforcement, see that the promise made to River's Edge residents is kept.

Commerce

Residents and visitors alike will find that the River's Edge has the best in shopping and entertainment. You'll also find lodging, restaurants and gaming. All these elements make River's Edge the premier economic hub of Rustifac.

Residential

The pride of River's Edge is her citizens! Those who call here 'home' have full use to the town's ammenities and hold jobs with salaries paid either by the town itself or it's local entrepeneurs. These quaint homes are sold by the town government and, once purchased, are property of the owner for life.
