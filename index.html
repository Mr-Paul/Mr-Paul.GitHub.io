Residence

The pride of River's Edge is her citizens! Those who call here 'home' have full use to the town's ammenities and hold jobs with salaries paid either by the town itself or it's local entrepeneurs. These quaint homes are sold by the town government and, once purchased, are property of the owner for life.

Commerce

Residents and visitors alike will find that the River's Edge has the best in shopping and entertainment. You'll also find lodging, restaurants, malls, bars and gaming. All these elements make River's Edge the premier economic hub of Rustifac.

Services

The sole purpose of the government is to provide for it's people. All fees assessed are returned back to the people in the form of public quarries, refineries and furnaces.
