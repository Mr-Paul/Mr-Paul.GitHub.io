Town Information

River's Edge was founded with the intention of bring all the people of Rustifac together. Whether making a permanent residence, trading at the various businesses, or to visit and meet other people, River's Edge will be a welcoming place for everyone. No reasonable faction will be declined entry and has the right to purchase a home or shop. We only ask that our town be treated as a sanctuary so that the people may go about their business in peace.

If River's Edge sounds like a place you'd like to reside or maintain a store, you can purchase a space from a town official at the standard rate.

Town Rules

  • General Rules
    • No theft, burglary or violence of any nature.
    • Weapons do not need to be holsetered but discharging a weapon for any reason other than self defence is not permitted.
    • Vandalizm or harrassment will not be tolerated.
  • Building Rules
    • No building structures of any kind without approval.
    • No placing deployables outside of your structure without approval.
    • No upgrading any parts of an original structure. You may, however, add walls, doors, etc. to the interior of residences and upgrade them as you see fit.
    • The master code for doors that are a part of the original structure will be kept by the town officials. Do not remove/replace these doors.
    • Only residents of the town (excluding hotel guests) will have access to tool cupboards in their district.
    • Custom structures will require their blueprints to be examined by a town official before construction begins.
  • Trade Rules
    • All items are to be valued in High Quality Metal.
      • You may sell items for High Quality Metal or you can offer High Quality Metal for items. You cannot, for example, sell stone for wood.
      • In-person trading, outside a registered business, is exempt from this rule.
    • Vending machines may be placed in approved/purchased locations only.
    • Vending machines must be stocked consistently. Machines that are neglected will have their broadcast disabled.
  • Town officials reserve the right to remove any person(s) who fail to follow these rules.
